Blueberry cake

Description

This is a simple recipe to prepare a tasty blueberry flavoured cake,its a very simple recipe that uses readily available ingredients and can be prepared by anyone. I hope youll enjoy trying out the recipe and bake yourself a delicious cake.

Ingredients

  • 2 cups all-purpose flour
  • cup white sugar
  • teaspoons baking powder
  • ¼ teaspoon salt
  • 1 cup blueberries
  • teaspoon lemon zest
  • ⅓ cup shortening
  • ¾ cup milk
  • 1 egg

Steps

  1. Preheat the oven to 350 degrees F (175 degrees C). Grease an 8-inch square baking pan.
  2. Sift flour, sugar, baking powder, and salt together in a large bowl. Combine blueberries and lemon zest in another bowl.
  3. Beat shortening in a third bowl until creamy. Stir shortening mixture into flour mixture, alternating with milk, then beat until well combined, about 2 minutes. Add egg and beat until incorporated, about 1 minute. Fold in blueberries, t hen pour batter into the prepared pan.
  4. Bake in the preheated oven until a toothpick inserted in the center comes out clean, about 50 minutes.
